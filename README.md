# SCDS Rating System
The synthetic content disclosure score (SCDS) rating system is a brand new way to inform clients and investors about generated content in your products.

## Get Rated
Learn about the rating system and complete the questionaire to get a rating on your product on the website:

**https://scds.igottic.com/**

## How It Works
A SCDS rating should roughly describe how much synthesis was used to make any piece of content, particularly relating to AI, but also including disclosures about automation and more. The score does not speak to the quality of the product, only how much synthetic material is in the product.

Scores apply to products, and a product can receive one of the following ratings:
* A+: No synthetic material used in the development or final product of content
* A-: Synthetic material was used in development but does not appear in the final product, or alternatively, synthetic material was used in the final product that does not use machine learning, LLMs, or other generative AI.
* B+: AI has a very minor role in the final product, but does not appear to the user or client.
* B-: AI has been used in the final product, but only partially; it is mixed with what is otherwise still mostly human-made content. Everything generated with AI is also based on human content.
* C: AI is used for entire features or parts of a final product, but some parts are still human-made.
* Q: AI is used for most of the final product, but is using or based on human-made content created during the development process.
* Z: The final product is almost entirely (or entirely) synthetic.

## How It's Calculated
The calculations for your ratings are based on a total score from questions, alongside a minimum and maximum rating. Question data, like scores, can be found in `questions.json`.
